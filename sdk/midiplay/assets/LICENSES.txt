midiplay asset licenses
=======================

Songs (all free to redistribute)
--------------------------------
entertainer.mid     Scott Joplin, "The Entertainer" (1902). Public-domain
                    composition; this typesetting from the Mutopia Project
                    (mutopiaproject.org, JoplinS/entertainer), distributed
                    by Mutopia as Public Domain.
wtk1-prelude1.mid   J.S. Bach, WTK Book I Prelude 1 BWV 846. Public-domain
                    composition; Mutopia Project typesetting
                    (BachJS/BWV846/wtk1-prelude1), Public Domain.
gymnopedie_1.mid    Erik Satie, Gymnopedie No. 1 (1888). Public-domain
                    composition; Mutopia Project typesetting
                    (SatieE/gymnopedie_1), Public Domain.
demo_groove.mid     Generated by tools/make_demo_mid.py for this project.
                    CC0 / public domain.

Patch banks
-----------
banks/GENMIDI.op2   DMXOPL3 by sneakernets (github.com/sneakernets/DMXOPL),
                    MIT license — full text below.
banks/allegro.ibk   General MIDI FM patches by Jorrit Rouwe, from the
                    Allegro library (liballeg.org, src/misc/fm_instr.h),
                    converted by tools/allegro_to_ibk.py. Allegro giftware
                    license — full text below. Melodic only; percussion
                    falls back to the .op2 bank.

Banks NOT shipped (loader supports them; bring your own file)
-------------------------------------------------------------
standard.ibk / drum.ibk   Creative Labs' SBTimbre banks — proprietary, not
                    redistributable. Found in old Sound Blaster driver
                    installs. Drop into assets/banks/ and `make pak`.
fat.opl / *.ad      Miles/AIL Global Timbre Library banks shipped with many
                    1990s games — copyright their publishers. Same deal:
                    drop into assets/banks/ and `make pak`.
*.tmb               Apogee Sound System banks (Duke3D etc.) — extract from
                    the game you own, drop into assets/banks/.
