RISC-V Stack FM

A from-scratch soft-CPU stack for the Analogue Pocket: VexiiRiscv (rv32im)
running bare-metal C at 25 MHz, 64 MB SDRAM, and a double-buffered 320x240
framebuffer scanned out of DRAM. Apps talk to a small HAL (video, timing,
input, audio) — the long-term goal is writing games in plain C against it.

This FM flavor adds a hardware OPL3 synthesizer (try fmdemo.bin — a
playable FM piano). Games detect it via the HAL and fall back to PCM on
the base core.
